Pizza Barbecue

  • Pizza Barbecue
  • Time: 10-15 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Meat 300 gr
Bacon 100 gr
Mozzarella 250 gr
Barbecue sauce 300 gr
Pizza mass 300 gr of flour

Step 1: Prepare a good mass.

Step 2: Combine all the ingredients listed before and put them on you mass, but carefully.

Step 3: Put the pizza in the oven, heat it up to 220ยบ C and wait 10-15 minutes until it bakes.

Step 4: Get it out from the oven and enjoy your meal!
